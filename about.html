even a small love
about.txt

a group exhibition examining the effects of previous, intimate relationships:

the participating artists seek to explore the confluence of memory and identity; the result is both an acknowledgement and examination of the residual effects these intimate shared experiences have left behind. the remnants of these intimate relationships and their lasting influences will be considered through a diverse scope ranging from photography to fiber art and mixed media.

this is the digital space/artifact collection of the artists involved.

email us your artifact in whatever format you desire (jpeg, mp3, mp4, text). we'll keep it safe for you here.
